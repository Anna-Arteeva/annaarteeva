Areas of Expertise

Leading Designers in a Cross-Functional Team Setting:

I strongly believe in the power of cross-disciplinary collaboration. I advocate for designers to develop strong partnerships with product, engineering, data, marketing, support, and sales functions, whether through joining cross-functional teams or building relationships outside of their bubbles.

Diversity and Multicultural Communication:

Statistics indicate that diverse teams are more innovative and yield significantly better results. However, managing across diverse cultural contexts is far more challenging than maintaining mono-cultural ones. Leveraging my international experience and a profound interest in the science of intercultural communication, I help teams navigate through cultural differences in an increasingly globalized and virtual world.

Holistic Customer Experience / Service Design:

End-to-end Customer Experience transcends interface interactions, encompassing Customer Acquisition, Customer Support, Content, Offline Interactions, and more. Designers, with their unique perspective, are ideally positioned to connect disparate parts of the puzzle. Recognizing and addressing blind spots, designers play a pivotal role in creating cohesive, cross-platform experiences that significantly contribute to enhancing overall customer satisfaction.

Strategic Product Design and Research, Workflow Design:

Navigating between the big picture and intricate details is a critical but challenging aspect of design work. I specialize in guiding teams to develop a sharp product/UX vision, articulate strategy, and implement step-by-step tactics while ensuring the delivery of high-quality assets through continuous delivery practices. My passion lies in obsessively crafting effective yet minimal processes to maximize output.
